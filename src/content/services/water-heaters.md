When your hot water goes out, that's a same-week problem, not a someday one. We install, replace, and repair traditional tank water heaters across Lancaster County, and because we're licensed plumbers, the water and gas connections are handled by the same team that sets the unit.

## Sized to your household

A tank that's too small leaves you short on hot water. One that's too big wastes money heating water you don't use. We size it to how much your home actually uses so you get steady hot water without overpaying.

## Repair or replace — the honest read

Not every water heater problem means a new unit. Sometimes it's a part, sometimes the tank is done. We find the real cause and tell you straight, instead of pushing a replacement you don't need. If a tankless unit makes more sense for your home, we'll walk you through that option too.
