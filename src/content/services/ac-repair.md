When your air conditioning quits, it's usually on the hottest day of the year. We've kept Lancaster County homes cool since 1961, and we treat a repair call the way we'd want ours treated — show up, find the real problem, and give you a straight answer.

## We diagnose before we quote

A lot of AC problems look the same from the thermostat but come from very different causes. Instead of guessing, we check the system properly — refrigerant, electrical, airflow, and the parts that tend to fail — so you're paying to fix what's actually wrong, not to swap parts and hope.

## Straight talk on repair vs. replace

If your unit has good years left and the fix is reasonable, we'll repair it and move on. But if it's old and you're calling every summer, we'll tell you honestly that a new system may cost you less over time. We're not here to sell you a unit you don't need, and we're not here to keep patching one that's done.

## Backed by our guarantee

Repair work is backed by our 1-year labor guarantee. If you're weighing a bigger job, financing is available through Synchrony. Call us and we'll get you scheduled.
