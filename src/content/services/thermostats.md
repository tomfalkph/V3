The thermostat is the one part of your HVAC system you touch every day, and a good one makes the whole system easier to live with. We install and service thermostats across Lancaster County, standard and smart alike.

## Set up right, not just mounted

A thermostat only helps if it's wired correctly and matched to your equipment. Because we're the same team that handles your heating and cooling, we make sure the thermostat talks to your system properly and does what you expect — even heat, steady cooling, no guessing.

## Honest advice on what you need

A smart thermostat is a nice upgrade for a lot of homes, but it isn't required to be comfortable. We'll tell you straight whether a simple, reliable unit does the job for you or whether the extra features are worth it. Either way, you get one that's installed right and set up to work. Give us a call and we'll help you sort it out.
