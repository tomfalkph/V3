## Whole-home humidifiers

Winter air in an older home gets dry — static, cracked woodwork, that never-quite-warm feeling. A whole-home humidifier works with your heating system to hold a comfortable humidity level throughout the house, not just one room. We'll tell you honestly whether it's worth it for your home.

## Air purifiers

If someone in the house deals with allergies or the air just feels stuffy, an air purifier built into your system can help clean the air as it circulates. We'll walk you through the options and what actually makes a difference for your home — no overselling.

## Thermostats and smart thermostats

The thermostat is the one part of your system you touch every day. We install and service them, standard and smart alike, and because we handle your heating and cooling, we make sure it's wired right and matched to your equipment. A smart thermostat is a nice upgrade for a lot of homes, but it isn't required to be comfortable — we'll give you a straight answer on what fits.
