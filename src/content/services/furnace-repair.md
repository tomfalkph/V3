A furnace almost always fails on the coldest night. We've kept Lancaster County homes warm since 1961, and when your heat goes out we do the same thing we'd do at our own house — get there, find the real problem, and tell you straight what it needs.

## We find the real problem

No heat can come from a lot of places — ignition, the blower, a bad sensor, a safety switch doing its job. We diagnose the furnace properly before we quote, so you're paying to fix the actual fault, not to swap parts and hope it holds.

## Honest repair vs. replace

If your furnace is sound and the fix is reasonable, we repair it and you're back to warm. If it's aging out, breaking down every winter, or showing a safety issue, we'll tell you plainly that replacing it may serve you better. We won't push a new unit you don't need.

## Backed by our guarantee

Our repair work carries a 1-year labor guarantee. If a new furnace turns out to be the smarter move, financing is available through Synchrony. Give us a call and we'll get you on the schedule.
