A leak you can't see is the kind that does the most damage — to your walls, your floors, and your water bill. We find leaks and repair pipes across Lancaster County, and we've done it as licensed plumbers since 1961.

## We find the real source

Water travels, so the wet spot is rarely where the leak actually is. We track down the true source instead of opening walls on a guess, which means less damage and a repair that fixes the real problem.

## Caught early saves you money

A small leak today is a repair. Left alone, it becomes rot, mold, and a much bigger bill. If something seems off, calling sooner almost always costs you less in the end.
