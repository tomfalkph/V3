Much of Lancaster County runs on septic, and a septic system isn't the place to cut corners. We install and replace septic systems for properties across the county, and we've been a family plumbing shop in Millersville since 1961.

## Installation and replacement

Whether it's a new system for a property that needs one or a replacement for a system that's failed, we handle the installation. We're licensed master and journeymen plumbers, so the plumbing side and the septic side are done by the same team, to code.

## Our own excavation capability

Septic work means digging, and we have our own excavation capability — so the excavation and the system go in together, handled by one crew. That's simpler for you than lining up a separate excavator, and it means one team stands behind the whole job.

## We know rural properties

Decades out on well-and-septic properties across the county means we understand how these systems fit the land they sit on. Our work carries a 1-year labor guarantee, extendable up to 10 years, plus manufacturer warranties, with financing available through Synchrony.
