Lititz is one of those towns people are proud to live in — a well-kept downtown, historic homes, and newer construction mixed in around them. That mix is exactly why the heating and cooling work here takes some care.

## Older homes, older systems

Plenty of Lititz houses still run older boilers and radiators, or ductwork that was added on over the years and never quite balanced. We've worked in homes like these for decades. We know how to fit modern comfort into a house with history without tearing it apart.

## The right system for the house

HVAC installation and replacement is our main work — furnaces, AC, heat pumps, boilers, and ductless. We're an authorized Amana and Rheem dealer. On an older home especially, we run a real load calculation so the new system actually fits the house, instead of oversizing it and short-cycling all winter. Plumbing repairs and upgrades, we handle those too.
