## A city shop's worth of city homes

Lancaster is the county seat, and a lot of its housing shows real age — dense rowhomes and twins, historic neighborhoods, tight lots where the old and the newer sit side by side. Our shop is just west in Millersville, so we're close by, and we've spent decades inside houses built like the ones on your block. That matters when a system needs replacing.

## Older heating, done right

Many Lancaster homes still run older heating — boilers and radiators, tired ductwork, a furnace tucked into a cramped basement. When we put in a new furnace, heat pump, boiler, or AC, we run a real load calculation and size it for your actual home, not a rule of thumb. Right-sizing a system for a house with history is most of the job.
